About Us

We are conveniently located for the romantic town of Gretna green. We are a freelance service so our brides and their wedding parties can relax, as we travel to your home or hotel and you don't need to leave your room until your ceremony. Established in 1999 we have grown to become a team of experienced stylists working together to create something special for each clients. We strive to offer a bespoke look, so whether it be classic, romantic, boho or vintage styles we work with you to provide stunning hair & long lasting make-up looks for all of the bridal party. We take great pride in the work that we do.

Being a make-up artist or stylist isn't just based on artistic skill but the ability to communicate with our brides in order to create something special.

"We got married a week ago today and coming from Northern Ireland I was a bit worried about my makeup because I couldn't have a trial as we arrived the day before the wedding but I needn't have worried at all!! Kirsty arrived at the hotel room promptly at 9:30 my bridesmaid had her makeup done first and it was flawless! I on the other hand never wear makeup so was worried I'd look too made up, but again after much reassurance from Kirsty via messenger we decided on how my makeup would be and I can honestly say I've never felt so pretty or had so many compliments in my life!! I can't recommend complete bridal enough absolutely fantastic artists."

Feeling good about yourself is key to feeling confident and beautiful on your wedding day. We like to create a relaxed atmosphere for you to enjoy, as we understand 'getting ready' is a big part of your big day.

Hair & Make-up Planning & Consultation - Free!

We understand that most brides won't be able to visit us before their big day, that's why we've tailored our consultation process to suit you:

  • Gather images of the styles you like and bring along as a "look book".

  • With that we'll tailor our professional advice to your wants and needs.

  • Hair: our experienced stylists will advise on what hair style will suit your face, your veil or your chosen hair accessories.

  • Make-up: a natural look, effectively applied to even out skin tone or your own 'must haves', we'll get you that look you want.
