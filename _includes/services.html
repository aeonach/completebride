Hair & Make-up Planning & Consultation - Free!

At Complete Bride we understand that the majority of brides do not live locally to Gretna Green and cannot visit prior to the big day - don't worry, we are here to help!

  • We recommend that you collect images of the styles that you like prior to the day so you can bring along a "look book".

  • We will then discuss your desired style and advise on what will suit you.

  • Our experienced Wedding Hair stylists will advise on what hair style will suit your face, your veil or your chosen hair accessories.

  • With regards to make-up, whether you indulge in a natural look, effectively applied to even out skin tone or if you wish us to use any of your own favourite "must haves" we can incorporate your requirements.
