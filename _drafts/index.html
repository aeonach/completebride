Hair & Make-up Planning & Consultation - Free!

We understand that most brides won't be able to visit us before their big day, that's why we've tailored our consultation process to suit you:

  • Gather images of the styles you like and bring along as a "look book".

  • With that we'll tailor our professional advice to your wants and needs.

  • Hair: our experienced stylists will advise on what hair style will suit your face, your veil or your chosen hair accessories.

  • Make-up: a natural look, effectively applied to even out skin tone or your own 'must haves', we'll get you that look you want.
